The Infinite Game Philosophy
Long-term model over one-hit thinking

Core VIP Session #2 theme: stop thinking about hitting a single home run and start thinking about building a catalog. The infinite game is one you're never trying to "win" — you're trying to keep playing.

Applied to digital products: your goal isn't to create the perfect product. It's to build a system that lets you create products faster than the market evolves. One bad product doesn't matter. A portfolio of 20, 30, 50 products with a few hits changes your financial picture permanently.

This is why Iman doesn't call it a business — he calls it an "income stream system." It's infrastructure, not a single bet.

Pricing Tier Strategy
How to price for conversions

VIP sessions go deeper on pricing than the public sessions. Key frameworks:
