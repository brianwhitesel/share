Goal: Understand his business pain points and whether he's in a position to pay for help — landing page, booking system, or anything else. Also be ready to talk about what you're building if he brings it up.
Ask him
How are you currently getting new clients? Word of mouth, Facebook, something else?
Do you have a way for people to book you online, or is it all texts and DMs?
What's the biggest thing slowing you down or taking up time you'd rather not spend?
Are you trying to grow the business, or is the current volume about right?
Have you thought about having a real website or booking page? What's held you back?
If he asks what you're building
You're building tools and pages for small business owners — booking pages, client portals, content systems.
You've already built tools for a photographer, a videographer, and a social media manager — working on a few clients now.
The goal is to make it feel like you have a team behind you, even when it's just you.
Keep it high-level — you can show him the booking page you already built for his business if the moment feels right.
